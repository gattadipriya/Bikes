/ Our Story

WHY WE RIDE

🚵

Founded in 2012 in Hyderabad, VELOX was born from a simple obsession — making premium cycling accessible to every rider in India. What started as a tiny workshop has grown into one of the country's most trusted bike destinations.

We don't just sell bikes. We build communities, fuel adventures, and help people discover the freedom of two wheels. Every bike we carry is hand-tested by our team of passionate cyclists.

  • Curated selection of 240+ premium models
  • Certified mechanics with 10+ years experience
  • Lifetime service warranty on all purchases
  • Ride fit sessions for every new customer
  • Community rides every Sunday morning
